ABOUT ME

Why? and Why Not?
Two guiding questions I've had throughout my career and my life.

The "Why" has kept me constantly curious about how things worked. It has led me to dive head-first into a variety of different industries, including higher education, tech, entreprenuership, restaurant, finance, and construction.

The "Why Not" has allowed me to continually question why things are done a certain way, and look for ways they can be done better.

It is this drive and curiousity that has led me to this current path at LearningFuze to pursue web-development.
